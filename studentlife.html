Students Life in Vivekananda college

Student life at Vivekananda Government Degree College is a blend of academics and simple campus experiences. The college provides a supportive environment where students focus on their studies while also participating in occasional cultural activities, seminars, and events. With a diverse group of students, it offers opportunities to build friendships and improve communication skills. Although the campus life is not highly vibrant compared to private institutions, students benefit from a less pressured atmosphere, allowing them to explore personal interests, develop skills, and prepare for their future careers.

Academic Flexibility

  • Provides a structured curriculum with limited flexibility, as courses mainly follow the university syllabus.
  • Offers basic choice in subject combinations within streams like BCA, BCom, and BSc.
  • Allows students enough free time outside academics to pursue additional skills, certifications, or personal projects.

Skill Development

  • The college organizes workshops and seminars to enhance students’ practical knowledge and awareness of current industry trends.
  • Various academic and cultural events are conducted, helping students improve communication, teamwork, and leadership skills.
  • Encourages students to participate in skill-based activities and programs, supporting their overall personal and professional development.

Festivals and Events

  • The college celebrates a cultural fest, where students participate in dance, music, drama, and other creative activities.
  • Technical fests and academic events are organized to encourage innovation, learning, and student participation in technical fields.
  • Sports Day is conducted to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and a spirit of healthy competition among students.

Internships and Industry Exposure

  • The college encourages students to take up internships to gain practical experience and exposure to real-world work environments.
  • Basic placement training programs, such as resume building and interview preparation, are provided to support career readiness.
  • Students are guided to explore off-campus opportunities and industry interactions to enhance their professional skills.
