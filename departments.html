Mathematics

The Mathematics Department at Vivekananda Government Degree College provides students with a strong foundation in core mathematical concepts such as algebra, calculus, statistics, and discrete mathematics. The department focuses on developing logical thinking, analytical skills, and problem-solving abilities that are essential for various academic and professional fields. Through a combination of theoretical learning and practical applications, it supports students in understanding the importance of mathematics in areas like computer science, economics, and data analysis, preparing them for higher studies and diverse career opportunities.

BCOM

The B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) program at Vivekananda Government Degree College is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in commerce, finance, and business principles. The course covers subjects such as accounting, business economics, taxation, auditing, and management, helping students understand the functioning of organizations and financial systems. It also focuses on developing analytical, managerial, and decision-making skills. The program prepares students for careers in banking, finance, accounting, and business, as well as for higher studies like M.Com or professional courses such as CA and MBA.
