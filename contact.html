Vivekananda government degree college (details)

Vivekananda Government Degree College was established to provide quality and affordable higher education to students in Hyderabad. The college is affiliated with Osmania University and follows its academic curriculum and guidelines. It has been reaccredited with an ‘A’ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), reflecting its commitment to maintaining educational standards. The college offers a variety of undergraduate courses such as BCA, BCom, BSc, and BA across different streams. It provides essential facilities including well-equipped classrooms, computer laboratories, a library, and other basic infrastructure to support students’ academic growth and overall development.
