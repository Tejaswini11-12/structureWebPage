About Our College

Vivekananda Government Degree College is a government institution located in Hyderabad that offers undergraduate programs such as BCA, BCom, BSc, and BA. The college focuses on providing affordable and accessible education to students from diverse backgrounds. It emphasizes academic learning along with basic practical exposure, helping students build a foundation in their chosen fields. While the campus facilities are modest, the college supports students in developing knowledge and skills necessary for higher studies and future careers.


History of Vivekananda government degree college

Vivekananda Government Degree College was founded to expand access to higher education for students in Hyderabad, particularly those seeking quality learning at an affordable cost. Inspired by the teachings of Swami Vivekananda, the college has focused on academic growth, discipline, and character building. Over time, it has broadened its academic offerings across arts, commerce, and science streams, adapting to the changing educational needs of students. The college has gradually built its reputation as a supportive institution that encourages students to pursue knowledge and develop skills for their future endeavors.
